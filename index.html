Tied with my passion to the game, I would love to discover interesting data and information about the game with the viewers reading this. Enjoy.

Demographics in NBA

Of course most NBA players would come from United States of America. However, as the game of NBA basketball progresses, so do the international players.

For instance, France has a total of 35 NBA players (Most NBA players in a country next to Canada and USA) and two of the generational talents include Tony Parker (Retired) and Rudy Gobert. One was a four-time NBA Champion and another was Three-time NBA Defensive Player of the Year.

Despite Greece having only 9 NBA players, one of the most inspiring Greek-Nigerian professional basketball player certainly made his mark by leading the Bucks to their first NBA championship since 1971. His name is Giannis Antetokounmpo and he proved that Greece is one country to watch out for.
The Size Factor
(Are you fit to be in the NBA)

"...You should get into sports like basketball with that height of yours" is a common quote some people hear every now and then. That is a reasonable statement in fact, professional basketball players must be tall to be competitive in the league. In the visualisation of the weights and heights of professional basketball players in 1996 to 2022 below, the average height of a basketball player is between 195cm to 210cm. The tallest basketball player was Elhadji Tacko Sereigne Diop Fall, a 229cm tall Senegalese. While the shortest player is Muggsy Bogues, with a height of 160cm. Another interesting fact that can be seen from the visualization is that the average weight of NBA players is between 90kg to 110kg.
Lebron, Kobe, or Jordan

Michael Jordan, Kobe Bryant and Lebron James were and still are the undeniably most influential NBA players.

Although Lebron James has the least won championships (i.e. 4 championships won compare to 5 and 6 championships won by Kobe Bryant and Michael Jordan respectively), statistically, Lebron James is unsurpassed in terms of personal scoring stats. (e.g. Average points per game is 27.1 compare to 24.2 and 25.3)
